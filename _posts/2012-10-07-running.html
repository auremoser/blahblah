Many people have asked me to come up with a running playlist . I’d probably run to electro-euro-pop, so I’ll spare everyone that episode and take a more semantic/inquisition approach. As a non-runner, I’ll parse out the many meanings of a “run” and make it maximally accessible. So this for all the runaways and the people who run with it, the people running after antelopes and everyone else racing to the finish. This is also for the great universal everyone, since we’re all running out of time.
